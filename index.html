Quay crane (QC) Inspection checklist
1. DOCUMENTATION & CABIN CONDITION
No. Item / Description Status (Yes/No/NA) Remarks
1.1 Operator's license valid and available
1.2 Valid Third Party Inspection Certificate available
1.3 Cabin cleanliness and visibility (glass clear)
1.4 Cabin controls and joy sticks functioning
1.5 Display monitors and indicators working
1.6 Wipers and washers functional
2. SAFETY DEVICES & INDICATORS
No. Item / Description Status (Yes/No/NA) Remarks
2.1 Emergency Stop Buttons functional
2.2 Anemometer (Wind Speed) working correctly
2.3 Anti-Collision Sensors operational
2.4 Load Cell / Overload Protection functional
2.5 Brakes (Gantry, Trolley, Hoist) functioning
2.6 Siren and Warning Lights functional
2.7 Limit Switches (Upper/Lower, End of Travel)
3. MECHANICAL & STRUCTURAL CONDITION
No. Item / Description Status (Yes/No/NA) Remarks
3.1 Main structure free from visible cracks/damage
3.2 Wire Ropes (Main Hoist, Boom, Trolley) condition
3.3 Sheaves and Drums condition
3.4 Spreader condition (Twist locks, Flippers)
3.5 Trolley Rails and Gantry Tracks free from debris
3.6 Anchoring / Storm Pins functional
4. ELECTRICAL & HYDRAULIC SYSTEMS
No. Item / Description Status (Yes/No/NA) Remarks
4.1 Electric Cables and Festoon condition
4.2 Panels and Junction Boxes closed/secured
4.3 Hydraulic hoses and fittings free from leaks
4.4 Oil levels (Hydraulic, Gearbox) adequate
5. GENERAL SAFETY & HOUSEKEEPING
No. Item / Description Status (Yes/No/NA) Remarks
5.1 Fire Extinguishers available and charged
5.2 Access Walkways, Ladders, Handrails secure
5.3 Lighting (Flood lights, Cabin lights) operational
5.4 Housekeeping on crane platforms
5.5 Warning Signs and Load Charts posted
5.6 PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) available
5.7 First Aid Kit available
